About us

We at Freestuff4you are dedicated to provide services that would be beneficial for those who want to donate their stuff easily by just making a post about it on our website.

How to upload?

To upload the things you want to donate you first have to register to our website. Then verify your account sent to your e-mail id. Then by clicking on the upload tab on the navigation bar you will be directed to a page where you will be able make a post.

How to get the donation?

If you want to get the donations uploaded by others you can click on the post to go to the product description page. There you can read about the details of the post. Contact details of the post owner will be provided there which you can use to contact the owner and confirm with him that you are willing to take the donation.
